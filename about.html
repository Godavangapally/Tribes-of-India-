TRIBES OF INDIA

Our Project is a research initiative seeking to highlight the tribal communities of India. Our Project seeks to answer the questions related to tribal people like their origin, livelihood and challanges facing by the tribal people.






A Small team but a Significant impact

Our Project is a small team of 3 members.In our endeavor to empower and celebrate the tribal communities of India, our small yet dedicated team passionately works towards creating meaningful connections, preserving cultural heritage, and fostering positive change. Explore our initiatives that leave a lasting impact on the lives of tribal individuals, bridging understanding and promoting inclusivity.
